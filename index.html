Our Identity

Founded in 2024, AD ON SCREEN is a small startup with a big mission: to help businesses get noticed. We set up screens in busy places like shopping malls, where your ads can reach a wide audience.

Managed by Sirpathi Gangesh, a Chartered Accountant, AD ON SCREEN makes advertising easy. With just a few clicks, your message can be displayed on our screens, helping your business connect with more people.

At AD ON SCREEN, we believe that advertising should be simple, effective, and help your business grow.

Our Capabilities

At AD ON SCREEN, we specialize in delivering dynamic and impactful digital advertising solutions. Our capabilities extend across a range of services designed to enhance visibility and engagement for your brand.

We have screens strategically placed in top locations, including malls, supermarkets, stores, and other crowded areas with high foot traffic. This prime placement ensures that your advertisements reach a broad and diverse audience.

From vibrant displays for special campaigns to consistent advertising presence, our advanced technology and strategic positioning maximize the impact of your messages. Discover how our expertise in digital advertising can elevate your brand and connect you with more customers.
